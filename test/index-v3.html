About Projects

Lisa Blunt

Hi, I'm Lisa. I'm a software engineer in DC.


I work on a contract as a 508-HTML Consultant with the Federal Reserve Board.

I build Section 508-compliant webpages for the Finance and Economics Discussion Series (FEDS) and International Finance Discussion Papers (IFDP) note portions of the agency’s main website.

Before that, I worked on a contract as a Web Content Manager with the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC).

There, I maintained webpages on the agency’s external and intranet websites.

And before that, I was a Federal Technology Consultant in the Systems Integration service line at Deloitte.

My former clients include:

  • Internal Revenue Service (IRS)I developed program- and project-level requirements to integrate Affordable Care Act tax provisions into the agency's new and legacy systems.
  • Department of Veterans Affairs (VA)I supported the redesign of the Veterans Affairs Program Management Center of Excellence (VA PMCOE) SharePoint intranet site by collecting, analyzing, and maintaining information regarding the site enhancement’s requirements gathering and development processes.
  • Commodities Futures Trading Commission (CFTC)I established a requirements gathering and standards implementation model for the agency to follow in launching internal applications.
